Event Description

The silicon microchips themselves might be cheap , but the whole dark boxes  are not .

Take off any metal object on your arms or fingers, or else you may fall prey to the charges while messing up with wires and if messing with those tangled wires doesn’t freak you and is a game of your realm, then try beating the best, in speed and in brains.

You need good brains and an eagle’s eye  because when you jump into the dark box one screw can put your life at  stake. You surely get awarded for right moves, but hell, you pay for your wrong moves.

 

Rules

  •  Team size must be of 2 members.
  • Use of internet, pen­drives and any sort of helping material is prohibited.
  • Any extra participants registered will be excluded from the event.
  •  Team members must reach the specified room/lab 15 minutes prior to the event's starting time.
  •  Further rules if any, will be announced on the day of event.

 

Prelims

  •  The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 25 questions.
  •  The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.
  •  2  teams will qualify for final round.
  • +3 for correct answers and ­-1 for wrong.

 

Final Round

  •  This is the practical round.
  •  Participants will have to find screws and different parts, from their surrounding and if they don’t find them and assemble them in a limited time, they will lose .
  •  Participants will be judged on the basis of speed and cable management.
  •  Damage or loss of any hardware part is responsibility of the participant, and he/she is liable to pay for it.
  •  Decision of judges will be final and binding.
