Event Description

You may fall, you may rise

But you win only, if you are wise.

Roll the dice, the snake-ladder waits

Not just plain lady luck,

But a stroke of knowledge and fate.

Things get tricky here, If you are lucky, even the snake might help you! Wanna know how then get yourself registered.

Snake Jam is a non-tech fun event. Even if the participants are unaware of technical things, this event is here to give them an opportunity to play and win different prizes. It's snakes and ladders but with a special twist.

Good knowledge allows the player to climb a ladder and get closer to the finish, while bad decisions make the player slide down the snake, which takes them farther from finish.

So get ready to hop on the giant snake-ladder flex with the biggest dice ever!

Rules

  • A team will comprise of 2 members. One to move on the flex and the other to throw the dice.
  • Four teams will play at a time.
  • Roll the dice to play the game.
  • Odd number comes no questions, move that many steps.
  • Even number comes  a question will be asked, if answered correctly then move that many steps else stay where you are.
  • To take the stairs you have to show your knowledge by answering a question.
  • Even if you get bitten by vices you can still survive if you have luck.
  • Decision made by the organizers will be final. Any kind of disagreement, argument or nuisance caused to the organizers may lead to the cancellation of the teams registration.
  • Fee entry -10 rupees per team.
