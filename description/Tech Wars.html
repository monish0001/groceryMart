Rules

  • Team Event. Team of maximum 3 participants are allowed. 

 There will be three rounds


  • Prelims: Participants will be provided with different tech related and aptitude based question in this round.
  • Visualisation: Questions based on the Logos, CEOs, Latest technologies, Brand Ambassadors and tagline etc. will be displayed and be ready for a thrilling surprise.
  • Company Gleam : Name of a company will be provided to each team and within a particular time period each team will search about that company and then there will be a quiz round on company's information.
  • After qualifying one round, participants will be eligible to participate in the next round.
  • Winner will be chosen based on the maximum points earned.
  • The judges’ decision will be final.
  • In case of a tie, a tie breaking rapid fire will be held.
  • Participants will be briefed with detailed rules during the event.
