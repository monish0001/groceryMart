  • Off side: ON
  • Time/Score Display: ON
  • Player Status Bar: Player Names
  • Camera: Default/Tele broadcast
  • Custom Formation-Disallowed
  • Custom Tactics: Allowed
  • Tactical Defending: ON
  • Legacy Defending: OFF PICKING TEAMS

  • Every player is allowed to pick their preferred team instead of WORLD XI and MLS ALL STARS. Players are allowed to use multiple teams throughout the tournament as they please.

The admin has the right to disqualify players from the tournament after misconduct or unethical behavior during the tournament. Disqualification means that the player is not allowed to play any more games in the tournament and will automatically lose every next game with the maximum score of 3-0.
