Event Description

Mime the hints, chase the imprints.

Hear the quiet, and you guessed it right!

It has been said that in the land of dumb, the man with a voice will be the king. Now, it's the time to prove them wrong because silence is going to be heard! A world without words would be like playing Tech-Charades, giving you various ways to express yourself. Yet, it wouldn't change the way you feel!

Would be played in a team of 2 among whom one has to act the given words and other has to guess it correctly.

Rounds :

Prelims

 

  • Daily used friendly technical words will be given. One has to guess maximum no. of words in 2 mins.

Final

  • A very easy question will be asked from categories - Bollywood, Proverbs in Hindi, General Knowledge and Sci-Tech. One of the team member has to answer the question and enact it to his partner. The team who answers and guesses 2 words in minimum threshold time wins the game.

Note

  • The team member who acts in prelims has to guess in finals and vice versa.
