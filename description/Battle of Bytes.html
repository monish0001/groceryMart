Event Description

Let the chroma of your coding capabilities come forth!!!

Do you believe your coding skills to be swift and merciless? Are you all for efficiency and for achieving the quickest execution time possible?

Then, come forth and resolve!

Rules

Round 1

  • It will be containing concept Based MCQ, One word and One Line questions.
  • It will be of 30 minutes and there will be 20 questions to be answered.
  • It will contain 2 sections of equal weightage. Section 1 will be C++ based and section 2 will be Java based.
  • Top 10 teams with maximum score will be qualified for next round.

Round 2

  •  It will be a de-bugging round of 1:00 hour.
  •  It will have 3 questions of both C++ and Java.
  •  Programmers will have only 1 hour for these questions.
  •  Marks will be rewarded for each corrected question at the end of event.

Note

  • It is a team based event. Each team can have a maximum of 2 members.
  • Use of internet, pen-drives and any sort of helping material is strictly PROHIBITED.
  • The teams must be present at the specified room/lab on the time.
  • Teams are not allowed to communicate with the other teams once the test starts.
  • The decision of the organizers shall be final and binding. Organizers have the right to disqualify any team if they break any Rule.
  • In case of a tie, the rankings will be determined by the sum of the total time elapsed when you submitted the correct submission plus a penalty of 3 minutes for every rejected submission (Wrong answer) for a problem you solved.
