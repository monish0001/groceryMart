Event Description

Got a nerve for developing Android applications? Then here's the battle you cannot miss
to fight. AppCombat is an Android Programming event in which enthusiastic App
Developers come together over a single platform to have a friendly battle over
applications.


Rules

  •  Each team will comprise of maximum 2 members.
  •  Competition comprises of 2 rounds

 Prelims

  • Duration 30 minutes
  • Prelims round consist of 30 MCQ’s.
  • 5-10 teams will be selected for the next round (Finals) on the basis of their performance in Prelims.

 Mains

  • Duration: 2/3 hours

  • In the final round each team will get Theme related question and they are required to strictly stick to the theme. The Application will be judged on parameters of both optimization and performance.

Decision made by the event organizers will be final and binding. Any arguments and/or nuisance caused to the organizers will not be tolerated.
